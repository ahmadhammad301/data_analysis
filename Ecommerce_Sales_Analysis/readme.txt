real-world data science, the data contains hundreds of thousands of electronics product purchases broken down by month, about 12 months worth of sales data.

starting with cleaning our data by:

* dropping nan types row
* removing some invalid cells
* change some rows types (to_numeric)
* adding multiple columns

then answering some business questions:

* What was the best month for sales?
* which product sells the most?
* Is there any relation between product price and quantity orders?
* What city sold the most product?
* Who are the best customers?
* what is the best time to boost advertisement?
* Is there any helpful relation between sales and weekdays?
* Is there any products sold together?
